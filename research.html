Boxiao (Beryl) Chen - Research

    Prof. Chen applies data-driven techniques from statistical machine learning and stochastic optimization to solve business related problems. Applications of her research include inventory control and supply chain management, dynamic pricing, assortment planning, retailing, energy and capacity expansion.
